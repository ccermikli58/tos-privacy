Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2025

1. Introduction

At InsightTrack, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Instagram Account Information: When you connect your Instagram account, we access your public profile information and analytics data.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including interactions, features used, and time spent.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase a subscription, we receive information about your purchase from Apple.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Analyze your Instagram data to provide insights
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services
  • Communicate with you about updates and features
  • Process subscriptions and transactions
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our terms

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Instagram and may use third-party services for analytics and payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them.

We use Apple's App Store for payment processing, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing your data
  • Request transfer of your data
  • Withdraw consent

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: ccgram.tr@gmail.com
